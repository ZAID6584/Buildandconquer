About Our Solution

Tackling urban congestion with cutting-edge AI technology

The Problem

Traffic congestion costs billions in lost productivity, increased fuel consumption, and environmental damage. Traditional fixed-time traffic signals fail to adapt to changing traffic patterns.

Our Solution

SmartTrafficAI uses computer vision and reinforcement learning to dynamically adjust traffic signals based on real-time conditions, optimizing flow and reducing wait times.
